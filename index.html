Statistical Arbitrage Model

Pairs trading is a strategy for hedging of risk, which involves matching of the short position with the long position in two underlying securities with a high positive correlation. This offseting of the long and short position is the basis for hedging, and provides profits even with market neutrality. This project analyzes potentially successful trading pairs by leveraging the python backtrader module.
